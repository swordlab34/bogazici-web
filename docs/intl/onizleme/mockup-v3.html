The work

Eight disciplines, each led by its own specialist

The dentist who plans your treatment performs it. Every restoration is milled and finished at our own bench — no outsourcing, no waiting.

01

Implantology

Single tooth to full arch. Planned digitally, placed surgically, restored from our own bench.

02

Aesthetic dentistry

Veneers, E.max, composite bonding, whitening and complete Hollywood smile design.

03

Crowns and bridges

Zirconia and lithium disilicate, milled in-house and finished by hand.

04

Oral surgery

Extractions, impacted wisdom teeth and gingival surgery, under local or general anaesthesia.

05

Braces and clear aligners, for adults and teenagers.

UK care · both ends of the journey

Through our UK partners you are looked after at both ends of the journey. A pre-consultation before you travel, and in-person attention at eight locations across the UK once you are home — so a concern is seen quickly, close to you.
